Twerking

It's an art form!

Letter from the Editor: Twerking is a dance that consists of hip thrusting and back bending. Its origin can be traced to the yoga, whose two poses cat pose and cow pose formed the basis of the modern day twerk. Twerking has quickly evovled from a dance to a powerful tool to communicate artistic, political and comedic ideas. I suspect in 10 years that twerking will be a necessary skill to include on your resume. So check back frequently for your latest twerktastic news.
